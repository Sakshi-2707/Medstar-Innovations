About Us

Medstar Innovations is a healthcare technology startup focused on developing affordable and advanced medical solutions for critical care. Our Automated Ambu Bag Resuscitator provides a cost-effective, portable, and reliable alternative to traditional ventilators. Designed for emergency and ICU use, it features precision controlled ventilation, battery backup, and manual override to ensure uninterrupted operation. Our goal is to make life-saving respiratory support accessible in low resource hospitals, ambulances, and disaster zones and for armed forces operating in tough climates with limited resources, where this lightweight, suitcase-sized device can be easily carried and deployed in critical situations.

  • The First Advanced, Low-Cost Automated Ambu Bag Resuscitator.
  • Designed for Hospitals, Ambulances & Disaster Relief
  • Ensures Continuous Ventilation Without Operator Fatigue.

Our device is NOT just another ventilator. It is a disruptive innovation that combines automation with affordability, making respiratory support accessible to all.

Founder's Message

Dr. Prashant Vishwakarma

Founder / CEO

While working in hospitals, I witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of ventilator shortages.

Patients struggled to breathe, and attendants were left manually compressing Ambu bags for hours—sometimes days—while waiting for ICU beds.

I knew there had to be a better way. This project started with a simple goal: to create a low-cost, portable, and automated breathing support system.

Today, that vision is becoming a reality.

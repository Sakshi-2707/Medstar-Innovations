Our Mission

Affordability & Accessibility

We are committed to bridging the gap between life-saving technology and the people who need it most. Our automated Ambu Bag Resuscitator is designed to be cost-effective and portable, ensuring that even low-resource hospitals, remote clinics, ambulances, and disaster-struck areas have access to reliable respiratory support.

Empowering Emergency Response

Time is critical during medical emergencies. Our mission focuses on equipping frontline workers, paramedics, and emergency caregivers with a dependable tool that can deliver consistent ventilation without the need for constant manual operation—saving time, effort, and ultimately lives.

Innovation with Purpose

We innovate with compassion. Every feature of our system—from precision-controlled ventilation to battery backup and manual override—has been engineered to meet the practical needs of underserved areas. Our goal is not just to build a device, but to build hope where options are limited.

Problem..?

• Shortage of Ventilators

• During emergencies, especially in remote or high-demand areas, ventilators are limited.

• Manual Fatigue - Healthcare workers or attendants must manually pump Ambu bags for prolonged periods—leading to exhaustion and inconsistent ventilation.

• High Cost of Commercial Devices - Most advanced ventilators are expensive and not feasible for wide-scale emergency deployment.

• Rural hospitals, ambulances, and disaster zones struggle with life-saving ventilation due to high costs and accessibility issues

Features

Discover the key features that make Medstar Innovations your trusted partner in healthcare technology.

Precision-Controlled Ventilation

  • Maintains accurate airflow to match patient-specific respiratory needs.
  • Adapts in real-time to changes in patient breathing patterns for consistent support.

Multiple Ventilation Modes (SIMV, PCV, VCV)

  • Offers flexibility to treat various respiratory conditions with tailored modes.
  • Enables mode switching as patient condition changes for optimal care.

Battery Backup & Manual Override

  • Ensures continuous operation during power failures with built-in battery.
  • Allows manual operation to maintain ventilation in case of system failure.

Portable & Easy to Use

  • Compact and lightweight for use in ambulances and field settings.
  • Simple interface enables quick setup and operation by medical staff.
